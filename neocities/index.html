In a world where prophecies used to be important and are now just a hassle for many- a seventeen-year-old human decides to profit off of that hassle with his odd ability of taking other people's prophecies and doing the dirty work for them- oh and they end up watching the world burn under the hands of a should-be villain-of-the-week but that's beside the point.

GENRE: slice of life + modern fantasy
STATUS: ONGOING!!!!!
CONTENT WARNINGS: unhealthy/dysfunctional relationships, murder, cannibalism, gore, mentions of sex, and insects

"Biram's Prophecy Service" is a self-indulgent OC project by two college students under the shared alis AWalBluff.
This site is mostly used for archival purposes so we don't lose track of who's who. It's also used as a convenient way for us to show our characters to the public.
